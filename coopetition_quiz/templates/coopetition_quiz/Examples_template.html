Example 1:

例1：

  • You can generate a large investment return by putting points in the Investment Account. For example, if both players invest all points in the Investment Account (\(x_A=20, x_B=10\)), the total return is \(\frac{1}{2}×20×10=100\) points. 
  • 你可以通过把资源置于投资账户中创造更大的共同收益。例如，如果双方把所有的资源都置于投资账户(\(x_A=20, x_B=10\))，共同总收益为\(\frac{1}{2}×20×10=100\)份。
  • However, it is risky to put all the points in the Investment Account. Suppose A puts 20 points in the Investment Account (\(x_A=20,y_A=0\)), and B puts 9 points in it and 1 point in the Rationing Account (\(x_B=9,y_B=1\)), the total return is \(\frac{1}{2}×20×9=90\) points. Because all the points in the Rationing Account come from B, B receives all the 90 points, while A earns nothing. 
  • 然而，把全部资源置于投资账户是有风险的。假设玩家A把20份资源置于投资账户(\(x_A=20,y_A=0\))，而玩家B把9份资源置于投资账户，在分配账户中放置1份资源 (\(x_B=9,y_B=1\))，双方合作总收益为 \(\frac{1}{2}×20×9=90\) 份。由于分配账户中所有资源都来自玩家B，因此B将独得90份资源，A将一无所获。


Example 2: The investment is profitable only if both players put points in the Investment Account.

例2：仅当双方都在投资账户中放置资源时，才有利可图。

  • If A puts 12 points while B puts 6 points (\(x_A=12,x_B=6\)), the total return is \(\frac{1}{2}×12×6=36\) points. 
  • 若A放置12份而B放置6份资源(\(x_A=12,x_B=6\))与各自的投资账户中，那么双方合作总收益为\(\frac{1}{2}×12×6=36\)份。
  • If A puts 12 points in the Investment Account while B puts 0 point (\(x_A=12,x_B=0\)), the total return is only\(\frac{1}{2}×12×0=0\), the same as when A invests nothing. 
  • 若A放置12份资源在投资账户，而B放置0份资源(\(x_A=12,x_B=0\))，双方合作总收益仅为\(\frac{1}{2}×12×0=0\),同理若A放置0份资源在投资账户。
  • Therefore, putting many points in the Investment Account is a waste if the other puts few. If you are not sure that the other will put points in the Investment Account, a safe option is to keep your points for yourself. 
  • 因此，若对方放置很少资源在投资账户可能导致你投资账户中的资源浪费。如果你并不清楚对方将会如何决策，较为安全的做法是将资源置于个人留存。
