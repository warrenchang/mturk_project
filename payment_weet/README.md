## Equality Equivalence Test (Kerschbamer, 2015)

This oTree app facilitates the implementation of the equality equivalence test to elicit individual level distributional preferences --- as proposed by Kerschbamer (2015), European Economic Review 76: 85-103 --- in different variants and parametrizations by simply altering the documented variables in config.py.
