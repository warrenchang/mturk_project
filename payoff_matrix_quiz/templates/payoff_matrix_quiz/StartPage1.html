In the following parts of the experiment, you will be randomly matched with another participant. You and the other participants will play an interactive game represented by the following payoff matrix.

Other participant
L R
You U a, e b, f
D c, g d, h

As shown in the payoff matrix, you have two possible actions: U or D, and the other participant has two possible actions: L or R.

The payoffs corresponding to the chosen actions are shown in each cell: the blue number represents your payoff, and the red number represents the other participant's payoff.

For example,

  • If you choose U and the other participants chooses L, then you receive a, and the other receives e.
  • If you choose U and the other participants chooses R, then you receive b, and the other receives f.
  • If you choose D and the other participants chooses L, then you receive c, and the other receives g.
  • If you choose D and the other participants chooses R, then you receive d, and the other receives h.

In the following page, you will be asked several questions to make sure you understand the experiment.
